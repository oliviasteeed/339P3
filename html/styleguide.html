Visual Identity

goals

This portfolio is intended to reflect me as a visual designer, graphic designer and art director. It conveys this message by focusing on two mainly visual design-based projects and explaining my process as a graphic designer. The website design itself also reflects my aesthetic of structural design with hints of fun.

type

Neue Haas Grotesk Text

Swear Display Black Cilati

The site title is written in Swear Display Cilati, conveying a hint of a fun and creative identity. This contrasts with Neue Haas Grotssk's utilitarian legibility which allows the project descriptions to convey context without competing with project imagery.

image

Project images are centered to contrast the left-justified text. Right-justified captions are used to balance the visual weight of text and images.

colour

Site colours are neon green, red-orange and offwhite. The bright colours convey a fun atmosphere without being overpowering, with red indicating interactability when links are hovered and green used subtlely as the highlight colour. The site background is offwhite to create a softer viewing experience and make the project imagery come to the forefront.
